FAQ

Questions we hear
a lot.

01How much does a website cost?
Most projects land between $4,500 and $14,000 CAD. Scope drives price. A focused 5-page brand site sits at the low end; e-commerce, custom CMS, or integrations push it up. We quote a fixed number after a 30-minute call so you never see a surprise on the invoice.
02How fast can you launch?
Two to four weeks for most small-business sites, kickoff to live. We hold a slot once a deposit clears and run a single design-build sprint, so things do not drag for months.
03Do I have to host with you?
No. You will always own your domain, your content, and your code. That said, most clients stay on our Cloudflare hosting because it is faster, cheaper, and we handle updates as part of the plan.
04Will I be able to edit the site myself?
Yes. We pair every build with a lightweight CMS (or a Notion/Google-doc workflow for simpler sites) and a 30-minute walkthrough. If you would rather not touch it, edits are included in our care plan, usually same-day.
05What about SEO?
Every site ships with clean semantic markup, structured data, fast Core Web Vitals, and local-business schema where it fits. We do not sell ongoing "SEO retainers". We build the foundation properly and point you at the few habits that actually move rankings.
06Do you do logos and branding?
Light brand work: yes (typography, palette, simple marks). Full identity systems: we will hand you off to a branding partner we trust. We are sharpest when we stay focused on the web.
