FAQ

Questions we hear
a lot.

01How much does a website cost?
Every project is custom priced. Scope drives the number: a focused custom build can start under a thousand dollars, while e-commerce, custom CMS, and deeper integrations sit higher. After a 30-minute call you get a fixed quote in writing, so the only number you ever see on the invoice is the one we agreed on.
02How fast can you launch?
Two to four weeks for most small-business sites, kickoff to live. We hold a slot once a deposit clears and run a single design-build sprint, so things do not drag for months.
03Do I have to host with you?
No. You will always own your domain, your content, and your code. That said, most clients stay on our managed edge hosting because it is faster, cheaper, and we handle updates as part of the plan.
04Will I be able to edit the site myself?
Yes, if you want to. Self-edit is a build-time option: we wire a lightweight CMS into your site so you can rewrite copy, swap images, manage FAQ entries, and reorder projects yourself. Saves push live in about a minute. Try the editor at wizardweb.ca/admin to see exactly what it looks like. For sites that do not need self-edit we keep the build static (faster, simpler) and handle changes ourselves on a flat care plan.
05Can you build me an AI chatbot or voice agent?
Yes, and it is one of our favourite things to build. Web chat, two-way SMS, and natural-sounding voice agents that answer questions, qualify leads, and book appointments around the clock. It is a custom add-on, not part of the standard build, and we design, train and host the whole thing. The chat bubble on this page is a live example, it even books the call itself. Try it, then ask us about yours.
06What about SEO?
Every site ships with clean semantic markup, structured data, fast Core Web Vitals, and local-business schema where it fits. We do not sell ongoing "SEO retainers". We build the foundation properly and point you at the few habits that actually move rankings.
07Do you do logos and branding?
Light brand work: yes (typography, palette, simple marks). Full identity systems: we will hand you off to a branding partner we trust. We are sharpest when we stay focused on the web.
