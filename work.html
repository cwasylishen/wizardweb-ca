16-Page Build

From an outdated Weebly site to a premium retreat experience.

Airborne Arts is a world-class flying trapeze and aerial arts retreat center in the mountains of Costa Rica. They run workshops, retreats, weddings, and private stays. The kind of place where your jaw drops on day one and you start planning your return before you leave.

They came to us with an old Weebly site that buried the story entirely. Visitors had no idea what was possible. We rebuilt the entire web presence from scratch: 16 custom pages, editorial design, and full SEO from the ground up. The site now competes at the highest level in the boutique retreat market.

  • 16 custom pages, each with unique hero imagery and layout
  • Hero image slider on the homepage
  • Embedded video showcase and Instagram feed integration
  • WhatsApp live chat for real-time booking inquiries
  • Google Calendar integration for retreat scheduling
  • FormSubmit contact forms on every inquiry page
  • Full SEO restructure with JSON-LD structured data on every page
